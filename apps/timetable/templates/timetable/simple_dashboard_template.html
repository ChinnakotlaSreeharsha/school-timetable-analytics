Getting Started

Welcome to the School Timetable Management System!


Next Steps:

  1. Visit the Admin Panel to add schools, departments, teachers, and classes
  2. Create time periods (P1, P2, etc.) and define your school schedule
  3. Add subjects and assign them to classes
  4. Start creating time slots to build your timetables

User Roles:

  • Admin: Full access to create, edit, and delete timetable data
  • Teacher: Can view schedules and limited editing rights
  • Student: Read-only access to class timetables
